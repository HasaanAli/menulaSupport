Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for restaurants?
Open the app and use the search bar at the top to find restaurants by name or location. You can also browse nearby restaurants using the location feature.
Can I view menus without visiting the restaurant?
Yes! That's what Menula is all about. Browse complete menus with prices, photos, and ingredient information before you decide where to eat.
I'm a restaurant owner. How can I add my restaurant?
You can add your restaurant directly through the app. Tap the "Add Restaurant" button and follow the steps to upload your menu, photos, and location details.
Are the prices accurate?
We work with restaurant owners to keep menu prices up to date. However, prices may change. We recommend confirming with the restaurant for the most current pricing.
How do I report incorrect information?
If you notice incorrect menu information or pricing, please email us at hasaan.ali2003@gmail.com with the restaurant name and details of the issue.
Is Menula available in my area?
Menula shows restaurants from our database plus nearby restaurants from Google Places, so you can discover restaurants anywhere. The availability of detailed menus depends on whether restaurants in your area have been added to our platform.
How do I update my restaurant's menu?
Restaurant owners can update their menus directly through the app. If you need assistance, contact us at hasaan.ali2003@gmail.com.

# MenuLa - Your Halal Restaurant Discovery App

Welcome to **MenuLa**, the community-driven app for discovering halal restaurants and exploring their menus in your area!

## What is MenuLa?

MenuLa helps you find nearby restaurants, browse detailed menus with photos and prices, and discover halal-friendly dining options. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or planning a special meal, MenuLa combines community knowledge with real-time restaurant data to help you make the best choice.

## How to Use MenuLa

### Getting Started

1. **Download & Open**: Install MenuLa from the App Store and launch the app
2. **Grant Permissions**: Allow location access for nearby restaurant discovery (optional)
3. **Browse Freely**: Start exploring restaurants and menus immediately‚Äîno account required!

### Browsing Without an Account

You can enjoy many features without signing up:
- Browse nearby restaurants from community and Google Places
- View restaurant details, ratings, and hours
- Explore menu items with photos and prices
- Call restaurants or get directions
- Search for specific restaurants

### Creating an Account

Sign up to unlock additional features:
- Add new restaurants to the community database
- Contribute menu items with photos and details
- Edit and manage your own restaurant listings
- Use the OCR menu scanner to quickly add items
- Help build a comprehensive halal dining directory

**To create an account:**
1. Tap the **Profile** tab
2. Select **Sign In / Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and create a password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Check your email for a confirmation link
5. Click the link to verify your account

### Adding a Restaurant

**Prerequisite**: You must be signed in

1. Tap the **+** button in the Restaurants tab
2. Choose to search Google Places or enter details manually:
   - **Google Search**: Search by name, select a restaurant, and we'll auto-fill details
   - **Manual Entry**: Enter name, address, phone, and location coordinates
3. Upload a photo of the restaurant (optional but recommended)
4. Select the currency used by the restaurant
5. Tap **Add Restaurant** to save

**Tips:**
- The app will check if the restaurant already exists to prevent duplicates
- Use your current location or enter coordinates manually
- Make sure the phone number is valid (10-15 digits)

### Adding Menu Items

**Prerequisite**: You must be signed in and the restaurant must be yours

1. Open a restaurant you created
2. Tap **Add Menu Item**
3. Choose your method:
   - **Manual Entry**: Type item name, description, price, and category
   - **Scan Menu (OCR)**: Take a photo of a menu to extract items automatically
4. Upload a photo of the dish (optional)
5. Tap **Add Item** or **Save & Add Another** to continue

**Using Menu Scanner (OCR):**
1. Select **Scan Menu** when adding a menu item
2. Take a photo of a physical menu or select one from your library
3. Wait for AI to process and extract items
4. Review the detected items with confidence scores
5. Select which items to add to the menu
6. Edit any details if needed before saving

**Halal Requirements:**
- Menu items are automatically checked for halal compliance
- Items containing haram ingredients will be rejected
- Ensure descriptions don't include non-halal items (pork, alcohol, etc.)

### Managing Your Content

- **Edit Restaurants**: Open your restaurant and tap the edit button to update details
- **Delete Restaurants**: Only you can delete restaurants you created (this also removes all associated menu items)
- **Edit Menu Items**: Update prices, descriptions, or photos anytime
- **Delete Menu Items**: Remove items that are no longer available

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an account to use MenuLa?

No! You can browse restaurants and menus without creating an account. However, you'll need to sign up to add restaurants or menu items.

### Is MenuLa free?

Yes, MenuLa is completely free to download and use.

### How does the halal verification work?

When adding menu items, our system automatically scans item names and descriptions for haram keywords (pork, alcohol, etc.) in multiple languages. Items flagged as non-halal will be rejected to maintain community standards.

### Can I add any restaurant?

You can add restaurants that serve halal food. The app checks menu items for halal compliance. If the restaurant already exists in the database, you'll be notified to prevent duplicates.

### What if my location isn't working?

1. Check that location services are enabled in your device Settings
2. Grant MenuLa permission to access your location
3. If you prefer not to share your location, you can still browse and add restaurants using manual coordinate entry

### How accurate is the menu scanner (OCR)?

The OCR scanner uses advanced AI to extract menu items from photos. Accuracy depends on photo quality, lighting, and menu formatting. We show confidence scores for each detected item, and you can review and edit all items before adding them.

### Can I edit or delete content I didn't create?

No. You can only edit or delete restaurants and menu items that you created. This ensures data integrity and prevents unauthorized changes.

### How do I report incorrect information?

Please use the **Contact Support** link in the app's Profile section to report any issues or incorrect information.
